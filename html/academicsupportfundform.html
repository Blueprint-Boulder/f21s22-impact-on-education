2021-2022 Student Academic Support Fund

**Round Three applications are due November 7th.**

**Round Two applications are currently under review with funding award notifications expected by October 29th.**

For Round Three, our final 2021 funding round, we opened up additional funding to specifically support supplemental learning and tutoring programs. We've seen the success of such school-driven programs in helping targeted students since last year, and we are prioritizing those requests in our final round.

In Round One, we were able to meet the needs of 24 applications to address needs including classroom materials and curriculum, ILC furniture, emergency academic help, and music instrument purchase and repair.

For more information about this program and eligibility, please visit: www.impactoneducation.org/what-we-do/grants/academic-opportunity-fund/

Please use this form/application to assess your need and align it with the most appropriate resource, whether it be Impact on Education or another BVSD or community organization.

Thank you for allowing us to continue our support of your students!
